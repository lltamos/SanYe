Have a question?

Find answers to FAQs here.

  • Is the free plan really free?

    Yes! You can make unlimited random introductions and send welcome messages to an unlimited amount of new hires, all for free.

  • How does billing work?

    Billing is done monthly by credit card for Premium, and you can start or stop anytime. Billing is annual for Enterprise packages.

  • What’s an active Donut user? Is it my whole team?

    Nope! We only bill you for users actively involved in a Donut program that month, whether they’re in a Premium pairing channel or an onboarding workflow. We have more information on active users here, or you can estimate your monthly price using our Pricing Calculator.

  • Do you offer a free trial?

    Your first 14 days of Premium are free, no credit card required. Subscriptions are month to month, and start at just $49 for your first 10 users, so you’re welcome to continue piloting with a small group for as long as you’d like.

  • Do you offer special pricing for nonprofits or early-stage startups?

    Yes we do! Reach out to s[email protected] to see if you’re eligible.

  • My team has some concerns around security and privacy. Who can address them?

    We’re right there with you. We know your most sensitive data lives in Slack, and we take that seriously. Please review our terms of service, privacy policy, DPA, and a summary of FAQs for more info on data access and security. Note: we’re unable to complete security reviews or questionnaires for free plans.
